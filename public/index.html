About this project

Since childhood, I've loved to sit down and improvise at the piano. It helps me process whatever is going on in my life and feel more grounded. I usually just play for myself but during this challenging time it feels important to offer my music to the world. May these simple pieces bring you a sense of peace, connectedness, and hope.
