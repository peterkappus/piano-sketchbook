About this project

For as long as I can remember, I've loved to sit down and improvise at the piano. It's a way for me to process whatever is going on in my life and feel more grounded. I usually just play for myself but during this crazy time of Coronavirus and global pandemic, it feels even more important to share these with the world, mistakes and all.

I've recorded these with a Kawai ES100 in my flat in London.

Do you have a favourite track or suggestions for a name? Get in touch!
